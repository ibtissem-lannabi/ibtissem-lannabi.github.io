About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Inria Center of Paris in Serena team. My position is part of a joint laboratory between Inria and IFPEN and I work with Eric Flauraud (IFPEN), Martin Vohalik (INRIA), and Soleiman Youcef (IFPEN). My research focuses on “Adaptivity (regularization, solvers, meshes) and a posteriori error estimators for the geological sequestration of CO₂ in the framework of the SPE 11 benchmark.”

I obtained my PhD from the University of Pau under the supervision of Jonathan Jung (LMAP and Inria of Bordeaux) and Vincent Perrier (LMAP and Inria of Bordeaux). My thesis topic is about the "Analysis of spurious oscillations problem of Finite Volume Methods for low Mach number flows in fluid mechanics".
